PFT HRCT 6MWT PROMs 25 PICO questions · 28 narrative questions · GRADE methodology · Eur Respir J 2026 Screening Diagnosis Monitoring Treatment Research gaps Which patients should be screened for ILD? (Narrative questions 1–6) SSc RA IIM MCTD SjD SLE Strong Screen all SSc patients using HRCT regardless of symptoms or risk factors. High ILD prevalence makes risk-factor-based gating unsafe. Low certainty · 26 observational studies, n=8898 · ATA-I, diffuse cutaneous SSc, higher mRSS are key risk factors Should PFTs replace HRCT for screening? (PICO 1–4) Against (Strong) Do not replace HRCT with PFTs (FVC/DLCO) for ILD screening in any CTD. PFTs missed 6–88% of ILD cases in SSc studies. Sensitivity varied 34–99% across CTDs — unacceptably variable. Low certainty for SSc, RA, IIM · Very low for other CTDs · 9 SSc studies (n=3191), 4 RA studies (n=287), 2 IIM studies PFTs and symptom assessment remain important for functional evaluation — they cannot serve as standalone ILD screening tools. Missing ILD = lost treatment opportunity + increased morbidity and mortality. Should lung ultrasound replace HRCT? (PICO 5–11) Against (Conditional) Do not replace HRCT with LUS — either extended (>18 intercostal spaces) or short protocol (≤18 spaces). Overall LUS sensitivity 94%, specificity 86% across 12 studies (n=971), but false-negatives still cause diagnostic delays and LUS is highly operator-dependent.
